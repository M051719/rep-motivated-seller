### PART 2: Build Intro (3-5 min)

**Timeline: 0-3 seconds**

5. **Drag gradient background to timeline:**
   - Find `gradient-blue-indigo.png` in media library
   - Drag onto **Video Track 1** (main timeline at bottom)
   - Click the clip on timeline
   - **Duration:** Set to **3.0 seconds** (adjust by dragging edge or typing in)

6. **Add logo:**
   - Find `RepMotivatedSeller_Logo_White.png`
   - Drag onto **Overlay Track** (above Video Track 1)
   - Position logo at **0 seconds** (start of timeline)
   - **Duration:** Set to **3.0 seconds** (same as background)

7. **Center and size logo:**
   - With logo selected, look at preview window (top right)
   - Drag logo to **center** of frame
   - Use corner handles to **resize** (make it fill about 60% of width)
   - Hold Shift while resizing to maintain proportions

8. **Add logo animation:**
   - With logo still selected, click **"Animation"** tab (right panel)
   - Click **"In"** animations
   - Choose **"Fade"** or **"Scale Up"**
   - **Duration:** 1.0 second
   - Preview by pressing spacebar

**‚úÖ Checkpoint:** Play first 3 seconds. Logo should fade in on blue gradient.

### PART 3: Hook Section (5 min)

**Timeline: 3-8 seconds**

9. **Add second background:**
   - Drag `gradient-blue-purple.png` to timeline
   - Place it **after** the first background (at 3 second mark)
   - **Duration:** 5.0 seconds

10. **Add hook text:**
    - Click **"Text"** button (left toolbar)
    - Click **"Default text"**
    - Text appears on timeline at current playhead position
    - **Move text to 3 second mark** (drag on timeline)
    - **Duration:** 5.0 seconds

11. **Edit text content:**
    - Click text in preview window OR click "Text" tab in right panel
    - Type: **"Facing Foreclosure?"**
    - Press Enter/Apply

12. **Style the text:**
    - **Font:** Inter Bold (if installed) or CapCut default bold font
    - **Size:** 80-100 (use slider or type in)
    - **Color:** White (#ffffff)
    - **Alignment:** Center
    - **Position:** Center of screen
    - **Animation:**
      - Click "Animation" tab
      - In: "Slide from bottom" or "Pop"
      - Duration: 0.5s

**‚úÖ Checkpoint:** Play 3-8 seconds. Text "Facing Foreclosure?" should animate in.

    - Import `icon-warning.png` or `icon-checkmark.png`

    - Position to left of each text line
    - **Size:** Small (50x50px in preview)

**‚úÖ Checkpoint:** Play 8-15 seconds. Problem statements should be clear and readable.

### PART 7: Add Music & Audio (3 min)

22. **Add background music:**
    - Find your music track in media library
    - Drag to **Audio track** (below video tracks)
    - Position at **0 seconds** (start of video)
    - **Duration:** Trim to **30 seconds** (drag right edge to 30s mark)

23. **Adjust music volume:**
    - Click music clip on timeline
    - Look for volume slider in right panel
    - Set to **-18dB** or 30-40% (should be background, not overpowering)

24. **Add fade in/out:**
    - With music selected, click "Audio" tab
    - **Fade in:** 1.0 second
    - **Fade out:** 2.0 seconds

---

### PART 8: Transitions (Optional, 3 min)

25. **Add transitions between sections:**
    - Click **"Transitions"** button (left toolbar)
    - Browse transition styles
    - **Recommended:** "Dissolve" or "Fade" (subtle and professional)
    - Drag transition to **between clips** on timeline
    - Place at:
      - 3s (intro ‚Üí hook)
      - 8s (hook ‚Üí problem)
      - 15s (problem ‚Üí solution)
      - 22s (solution ‚Üí CTA)
    - **Duration:** 0.3-0.5 seconds each

### PART 9: Final Polish (2 min)

26. **Review entire video:**

27. **Check these elements:**
    - [ ] All text is readable (not too small)
    - [ ] Music isn't too loud
    - [ ] Transitions are smooth

### PART 10: Export Video (3 min)

29. **Export settings:**
    - Click **"Export"** button (top right)
    - **Settings:**
      - Resolution: **1080p** (1080 x 1080)
      - Frame rate: **30 FPS**
      - Format: **MP4**
      - Quality: **High** or **Best**
      - Codec: H.264

    - **Save location:** Choose your Videos folder or Desktop

30. **Start export:**
    - Click **"Export"** button
    - Wait for processing (1-3 minutes)
    - **Success!** Video file is ready

## üÜò TROUBLESHOOTING

**Video looks pixelated:**
- Export at 1080p, not 720p
- Check "Best" quality setting

**Music is too loud:**
- Reduce volume to -18dB or lower
- Ensure fade in/out is applied

**Text is cut off:**
- Ensure text is within "safe area" (not too close to edges)
- Reduce font size if needed

**Export takes forever:**
- Normal for first export (builds cache)
- Subsequent exports will be faster
- Close other programs to free up RAM

**Can't find imported assets:**
- Check media library (left panel)
- Re-import if needed
- Ensure file paths are correct
