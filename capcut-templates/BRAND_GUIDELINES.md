## Typography

### Primary Font: **Inter** (Sans-serif)
- **Use for:** Headings, titles, body text, UI elements
- **Download:** Google Fonts or system default
- **Weights:**
  - Light (300) - Subheadings
  - Regular (400) - Body text
  - Medium (500) - Emphasis
  - Semibold (600) - Section headers
  - Bold (700) - Main headings
  - Extrabold (800) - Hero text

### Secondary Font: **Poppins** (Alternative sans-serif)
- **Use for:** Decorative headings, special callouts
- **Weights:** 600 (Semibold), 700 (Bold)

### Text Hierarchy in Videos

**Hero/Main Title**
- Font: Inter Extrabold (800)
- Size: 72-96px (relative to 1080p)
- Color: White or Blue-600
- Shadow: 0px 4px 20px rgba(0,0,0,0.3)

**Section Headers**
- Font: Inter Bold (700)
- Size: 48-64px
- Color: Indigo-700 or White
- Letter spacing: -0.02em

**Body Text / Subtitles**
- Font: Inter Semibold (600)
- Size: 32-42px
- Color: Gray-700 or White
- Line height: 1.5

**Small Text / Captions**
- Font: Inter Medium (500)
- Size: 24-28px
- Color: Gray-600 or White with 80% opacity

## Logo Usage

### Primary Logo
- **File:** `RepMotivatedSeller_Logo_Primary.png`
- **Dimensions:** 2000x600px (transparent background)
- **Use for:** Intros, outros, watermarks
- **Placement:** Top-left or bottom-right corner
- **Size:** 15-20% of video width
- **Padding:** Minimum 40px from edges

### Logo Variations
1. **Full Color** - Use on white/light backgrounds
2. **White** - Use on dark backgrounds or over video
3. **Blue Monochrome** - Use for minimal aesthetic
4. **Icon Only** - Use for small watermarks (250x250px)

### Minimum Clear Space
- Maintain clear space equal to the height of the "R" in RepMotivatedSeller
- Never stretch, compress, or rotate the logo

## Video Template Specifications

### Social Media Posts (Instagram/Facebook)

**Aspect Ratios:**
- Square: 1:1 (1080x1080)
- Portrait: 9:16 (1080x1920) - Stories/Reels
- Landscape: 16:9 (1920x1080) - Feed videos

**Duration:** 15-60 seconds

**Structure:**
1. **Intro (2-3s):** Logo reveal with gradient background
2. **Hook (3-5s):** Bold text with key question/statement
3. **Content (30-45s):** Main message with supporting visuals
4. **CTA (5-8s):** Call-to-action with contact info
5. **Outro (2-3s):** Logo + tagline

**Animation Style:**
- Smooth fade-ins (0.5s)
- Slide transitions (0.3s)
- Text animations: Typewriter or fade-up
- Avoid excessive motion - keep it professional

---

### Educational Content

**Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 (1920x1080)

**Duration:** 2-10 minutes

**Structure:**
1. **Intro (5-8s):**
   - Animated logo
   - Title card with episode/chapter number
   - Topic introduction

2. **Content Sections:**
   - Lower third name plates
   - Chapter markers
   - Key point callouts
   - Statistic highlights

3. **B-Roll Overlays:**
   - 70% opacity for text overlays
   - Subtle zoom animations (1.0x to 1.1x over 5s)

4. **Outro (8-10s):**
   - Summary points
   - Next steps CTA
   - Subscribe/Follow graphics
   - Contact information

**Color Scheme:**
- Background: White or Light Blue gradient
- Text: Dark Blue, Indigo, Black
- Highlights: Purple or Green (for positive points)

---

### Testimonials/Stories

**Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 (1920x1080) or 9:16 (1080x1920)

**Duration:** 30-90 seconds

**Structure:**
1. **Setup (5s):**
   - Soft background music fade-in
   - Subject name + location text overlay
   - "Success Story" badge

2. **Story (60-75s):**
   - Minimal text overlays
   - Pull quotes highlighted
   - Emotional music

3. **Result (10s):**
   - Success metric callout
   - Happy ending shot
   - Logo + CTA

**Visual Style:**
- Warm, hopeful tone
- Soft focus transitions
- Golden hour lighting (if possible)
- Minimal text - let the story speak

**Text Overlays:**
- Client Name: Inter Bold, 42px, White with subtle shadow
- Pull Quotes: Inter Semibold, 56px, White, centered
- Background: Dark overlay (40% opacity) for text readability

---

### Marketing/Ads

**Aspect Ratios:**
- Square: 1:1 (1080x1080) - Meta Ads
- Vertical: 9:16 (1080x1920) - Stories, TikTok
- Horizontal: 16:9 (1920x1080) - YouTube Pre-roll

**Duration:** 15-30 seconds (max)

**Structure:**
1. **Hook (2-3s):**
   - Attention-grabbing question or statistic
   - Bold, large text
   - Eye-catching visual

2. **Problem (5-8s):**
   - Identify pain point
   - Relatable scenario
   - Empathetic tone

3. **Solution (8-10s):**
   - Introduce RepMotivatedSeller
   - Key benefits (3 max)
   - Visual proof

4. **CTA (5-7s):**
   - Clear action: "Get Help Now", "Book Free Consultation"
   - Website URL or phone number
   - Urgency: "Don't wait - act today"

**Pacing:**
- Fast cuts (2-3s per scene)
- Upbeat background music
- High energy but professional

## Motion Graphics & Animations

### Logo Animations

**Intro Animation (3 seconds):**
1. Start: Logo elements separate and blurred
2. 0.5s: Elements snap into position
3. 1.0s: Color fills from left to right (gradient sweep)
4. 1.5s: Tagline fades in below
5. 2.5s: Full logo with subtle glow
6. 3.0s: Hold

**Outro Animation (2 seconds):**
1. Start: Full logo on screen
2. 0.5s: Fade down to 50% opacity
3. 1.0s: Scale down to 80%
4. 1.5s: Fade to black
5. 2.0s: Complete

### Text Animations

**Title Reveal:**
- Type: Slide up + Fade in
- Duration: 0.8s
- Easing: Ease-out
- Offset: 40px from bottom

**Callout Box:**
- Type: Scale from center
- Duration: 0.5s
- Easing: Bounce (subtle)
- Scale: 0 → 1.0

**Statistics Counter:**
- Type: Number count-up
- Duration: 1.5s
- Easing: Linear
- Format: Add commas, $ symbols

### Transitions

**Scene Transitions:**
- **Fade:** 0.5s (default between scenes)
- **Cross Dissolve:** 0.8s (for similar content)
- **Wipe:** 0.6s (for chapter changes)
- **Zoom Transition:** 1.0s (for emphasis)

**Avoid:** Harsh cuts, spinning, flips, excessive motion blur

## Music & Audio

### Music Selection

**Educational Videos:**
- Genre: Soft corporate, ambient, inspiring
- Tempo: 80-110 BPM
- Mood: Hopeful, trustworthy, professional
- Volume: -24dB (background)

**Social Media:**
- Genre: Upbeat pop, motivational
- Tempo: 120-140 BPM
- Mood: Energetic, positive, engaging
- Volume: -18dB

**Testimonials:**
- Genre: Emotional piano, strings, acoustic
- Tempo: 60-80 BPM
- Mood: Heartfelt, sincere, warm
- Volume: -28dB (very subtle)

**Marketing/Ads:**
- Genre: Energetic corporate, modern pop
- Tempo: 130-150 BPM
- Mood: Urgent, exciting, confident
- Volume: -20dB

### Voice Over Guidelines

**Tone:** Professional, empathetic, reassuring

**Pacing:** 140-160 words per minute (conversational)

**Recording Quality:**
- Sample Rate: 48kHz
- Bit Depth: 24-bit
- Format: WAV or FLAC
- Noise Floor: Below -60dB

**Post-Processing:**
- EQ: Reduce mud (200-400Hz), boost presence (2-4kHz)
- Compression: 3:1 ratio, -10dB threshold
- De-esser: Target 6-8kHz sibilance
- Normalization: -3dB peak

## Lower Thirds & Graphics

### Standard Lower Third (Name Plate)

**Dimensions:** 600x120px (positioned at 1/3 from bottom)

**Design:**
- Background: Blue-600 to Indigo-700 gradient (90°)
- Border: None or 2px white outline
- Text:
  - Name: Inter Bold, 32px, White
  - Title: Inter Medium, 24px, White 80% opacity
- Padding: 20px horizontal, 15px vertical
- Corner radius: 8px

**Animation:**
- In: Slide from left + Fade (0.6s)
- Hold: 5-8 seconds
- Out: Fade (0.4s)

### Chapter Markers

**Design:**
- Position: Top center or left
- Background: Purple-600 with 90% opacity
- Text: "Chapter 3: Mortgage Modification" - Inter Semibold, 28px
- Icon: Matching emoji or simple icon on left
- Padding: 12px horizontal, 8px vertical

## Best Practices

### Do's ✅
- Use consistent brand colors throughout
- Keep text on screen for minimum 3 seconds
- Add captions/subtitles for accessibility
- Use high-quality images (1080p minimum)
- Maintain 16:9 safe zones (5% margin from edges)
- Test on mobile devices before publishing
- Include clear CTAs in every video
- Use royalty-free music from approved sources

### Don'ts ❌
- Don't use more than 3 fonts in one video
- Don't use Comic Sans or overly decorative fonts
- Don't stretch or distort the logo
- Don't use harsh transitions or excessive effects
- Don't play music louder than voice-over
- Don't use low-resolution images or logos
- Don't forget captions for accessibility
- Don't violate copyright (music, images, clips)
