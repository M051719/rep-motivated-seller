## FAQ Preparation

**Q: "Can I do a short sale if I'm already in foreclosure?"**
A: "Yes! You can pursue a short sale right up until the foreclosure sale date. The earlier you start, the better, but it's rarely too late."

**Q: "Won't a short sale still hurt my credit?"**
A: "Yes, but significantly less than foreclosure. Think of it as choosing between a broken bone vs. a bruise - both hurt, but one heals much faster."

**Q: "How long does a short sale take?"**
A: "Typically 60-120 days depending on your lender. We work directly with lenders to expedite the process."

**Q: "Will I owe taxes on forgiven debt?"**
A: "Possibly, but the Mortgage Forgiveness Debt Relief Act may protect you. This is something we help you understand during consultation."

**Q: "Do I need a realtor for a short sale?"**
A: "As cash buyers, we handle everything directly, including negotiating with your lender. No realtor fees, no commissions."
